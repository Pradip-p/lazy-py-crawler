Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 21, 2025

1. Data Ownership

The data you scrape using Lazy Crawler is yours. We do not store or mirror your scraped results unless you are explicitly using our managed Pro hosting. For open-source users, all data remains in your local MongoDB instance.

2. Compliance

Users are responsible for ensuring that their scraping activities comply with the Target website's Terms of Service and local data protection laws (GDPR, CCPA, etc.).

3. Security

We take the security of your scraping configurations seriously. API keys and credentials should be managed via environment variables and are never stored in plain text by our system.
