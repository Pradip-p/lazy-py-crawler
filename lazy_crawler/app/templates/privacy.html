Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 27, 2025

1. Introduction

Crawlio Intelligence ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our web scraping platform and services, particularly for our clients in Australia, the United States, and globally.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us when you register for an account, request a consultation, or use our services. This may include:

  • Account Information: Name, professional email address, company name, and contact details.
  • Technical Data: IP addresses, browser type, and usage patterns when accessing our dashboard.
  • Service Data: Configurations, target URLs, and metadata related to your scraping projects.

3. Data Ownership & Processing

Your Data is Yours. Any data extracted or processed through Crawlio Intelligence remains your exclusive property. We do NOT:

  • Sell or commercialize your extracted insights.
  • Mirror your data for our own use.
  • Use your proprietary data to train AI models without explicit consent.

4. International Compliance

We adhere to the highest global standards for data protection:

  • Australia: We comply with the Australian Privacy Act 1988 and the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs).
  • USA: we adhere to state-specific regulations including the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA).
  • EU/UK: We maintain GDPR readiness for all data related to residents of the European Economic Area.

5. Data Security

We employ SOC 2 Type II compliant security protocols. This includes end-to-end encryption for data in transit and at rest, regular penetration testing, and strict access controls via encrypted environment variables.

6. Cookies and Tracking

We use functional cookies to maintain your session and analyze dashboard performance. You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. To exercise these rights, or if you have questions about our Australian Privacy Act compliance, please contact our Data Protection Officer.

8. Contact Us

For privacy-related inquiries, please email: lazycrawler@gmail.com
